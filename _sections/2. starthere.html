This is a (nearly) comprehensive guide to surviving and succeeding in medical school. Why should I listen to you?

Hmm... I haven't figured that one out yet. In all seriousness though, that's a great question. This is all just one person's opinion. I will present as much evidence as I can to explain why I do things the way I do, but it is up to you to try different methods before picking something that works best for you.

The advice given here comes from my personal experience as well as some extensive googling. I do not endorse blindly following everthing written here (or anywhere else on the internet). Try out some of the things I write about, talk to your fellow classmates and upperclassmen about what they recommend, and find something that works for you.
